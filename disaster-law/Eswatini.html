NATIONAL SOCIETY LEGAL FACILITIES



The Baphalali Swaziland Red Cross Society Act of 1970 (BERCS Act) provides the legal foundation of BERCS and recognizes the auxiliary role of the National Society.There do not appear to be explicit provisions which provide legal facilities related to international disaster assistance to BERCS under the law. The National Society should be contacted directly to determine whether any legal facilities are accessible by the National Society in the country.

For support please contact the National Society in this regard or contact the IFRC Africa Disaster Law Team: stella.ngugi@ifrc.org.

LEGAL FACILITIES FOR INTERNATIONAL ASSISTING ACTORS


The Disaster Management Act of 2006 does not provide legal facilities for international assisting actors.

In terms of sectoral laws, Schedule 4 of the Customs and Excise Act of 1971 provides that goods imported for the relief of distress of persons in cases of famine or other national disaster may be imported under a rebate of customs duty (Item 41211) provided they have an ITAC certificate. Such goods are also exempted from requiring an import permit in terms of the Import Control Regulations of 1980 and are also exempt from VAT in terms of the Value Added Tax Act of 2011.

For further information, refer to Legal Preparedness for International Disaster Assistance in Eswatini: Mapping of Domestic Laws and Policies which identifies the sectoral laws generally applicable to various aspects of international disaster relief in Angola and examines whether there are any provisions which may indirectly facilitate international disaster assistance.For support in contacting the National Society in this regard, please contact the IFRC Africa Disaster Law Team: stella.ngugi@ifrc.org.

IDRL INSTRUMENTS

IFRC Disaster Law is globally recognized as a leader in International Disaster Response Law (IDRL), and has developed a suite of tools to support the development of laws and policies which mitigate the common regulatory barriers which arise in international disaster response operations:

  • The Guidelines for the Domestic Facilitation and Regulation of International Disaster Relief and Initial Recovery Assistance (IDRL Guidelines).

  • The Model Act for the Facilitation and Regulation of International Disaster Relief and Initial Recovery Assistance (IDRL Model Act),.

  • The Model Emergency Decree for the Facilitation and Regulation of International Disaster Relief and Initial Recovery Assistance (IDRL Model Emergency Decree).

  • The Checklist on the Facilitation and Regulation of International Disaster Relief and Initial Recovery Assistance  (IDRL Checklist).
